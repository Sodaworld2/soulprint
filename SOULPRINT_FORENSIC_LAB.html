OCT Craquelure Analysis Authentic age cracks are rectangular in cross-section. Forged cracks are triangular. Like fingerprints — unique.

FIRE: Frequency-Guided Reconstruction

CVPR 2025 · OPEN SOURCE

The most important AI detection method. Diffusion models struggle to reconstruct mid-band frequency information in real images. Extract frequencies, run through diffusion model, compare reconstruction error. Real = high error. AI = low error.

Generalises to unseen diffusion models. Robust against perturbations. Open source: github.com/Chuchad/FIRE

FOURIER ANALYSIS · >99% ACCURACY

Fast Fourier Transform converts the image to frequency domain. ALL GAN architectures leave spectral artifacts from upsampling: CycleGAN/StarGAN produce dots and lines; StyleGAN shows mid/high-frequency anomalies. Over 99% detection accuracy.

Error Level Analysis (ELA)

JPEG FORENSICS · BROWSER-NATIVE

Re-compress the JPEG and compute per-pixel difference. Uniform = authentic compression history. Bright spots = regions with different compression cycles = edits, pastes, or AI generation artifacts. Runs entirely in the browser.

Already implemented at 29a.ch using HTML5 Canvas. Real-time rendering with adjustable quality sliders.

Add noise to the image, then use a diffusion model to denoise. Real images and generated images "snap back" to different manifold positions. The reconstruction distance reveals whether the image was AI-generated.
